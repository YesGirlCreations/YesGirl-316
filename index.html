My Story

“Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation. The old has passed away; behold, the new has come.”
— 2 Corinthians 5:17

I am a trauma survivor, but my story does not end with trauma. Jesus rescued me, saved me, and showed me that I am chosen, loved, and forgiven.

This website is not about me. It is about the God who never gave up on me. My prayer is that my story points you to Jesus — the One who restores, heals, gives hope, and calls each of us to take the next step of faith.

If He could change my life, He can meet you where you are too. 💜
