🌸 My Story

“Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation. The old has passed away; behold, the new has come.”
— 2 Corinthians 5:17
← Back to Home

I am a trauma survivor.

I have lived a turbulent life. I have struggled with addiction, experienced broken bones, heartbreak, betrayal, and more. I am a sinner. I have made many choices that I am not proud of, and there have been times when I faced the consequences of my own actions.

But Jesus rescued me.

He saved me. He changed my perspective on life. He showed me that I am chosen, I am loved, and I am forgiven. By His amazing grace, He made me new.

And it all began with one simple word... Yes.

My name is Amber Bostic, and I am God's Yes Girl. When God says, "Step," I choose to say, "Yes."

“I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.”
— Philippians 4:13

My story is far from over. I know there will be more trials, but I also know I will never face them alone because my life is rooted in Jesus Christ. He has never left me. He never will.

My greatest hope is to help you say yes to Him too.

If my story points you to me, then I have missed the point. My prayer is that it points you to Jesus—the One who restores, heals, gives hope, and lovingly calls each of us to take the next step of faith.

This website is not about me.

It is about the God who never gave up on me.

My prayer is that through every page, every journal entry, every song, every book, and every creation, you will come to know the incredible love, grace, and hope that can only be found in Jesus Christ.

He changed my life, and I believe He can change yours too.

I love you, no matter what.

With love,
Amber Bostic
God's Yes Girl 🦋
